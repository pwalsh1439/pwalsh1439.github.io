Ofsted Reporting

Ofsted is the Office for Standards in Education, Children’s Services and Skills. We inspect and regulate services that care for children and young people, and services providing education and skills for learners of all ages.Ofsted - What we do

This site was created to provide a simple Geospatial representation of establishments Ofsted ratings. With a goal of making the information more accessible thus enabling users to make informed decisions.

To provide simple visualisation of an establishments Ofsted rating a traffic light system is used to distinguish between rating levels.

  • Inadequate
  • Requires improvement
  • Good
  • Outstanding

Ofsted data has been taken from Data View which presents a point-in-time dataset and is available free of charge under the Open Government Licence v3.0.
